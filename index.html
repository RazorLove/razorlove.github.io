Available Templates

  • Foocoin: The original template, Scrypt hashcash algorithm with Proof of Work, based on Litecoin
  • ABCCoin: SHA256 hashcash with PoW, standard Bitcoin clone
  • Fehcoin: Scrypt hashcash with PoW/PoS (Proof of Stake), based on Litecoin and Peercoin
  • Blehcoin: SHA256 hashcash with PoW/PoS, should be based on Peercoin but seems to be based on Cosmoscoin. I'll need to figure that out.

Incomplete Templates

  • Tehcoin: Multihash template based on Quark
  • Fuhcoin: Prime chain PoW template based on Primecoin
  • Modcoin: Yet to be released modular template.
